
'a' : increase the angle of the canon
'b' : decrease the angle of the canon
'f' : makes ball faster
's' : makes ball slower
'SPACE' : shoot the ball
moving the mouse on screen helps in changing the angle
scrolling up/down increases/decreases the speed of throw.
left click shoots the ball

You are given five balls to shoot. Your aim is to hit the circular target as many times as you can to earn more score.

score and number of turns left will be shown on terminal

power will be indicated by a rectangle on left hand side of the screen which changes color as the power is increased/decreased.
